About Me

Modupeoreoluwa Olotu

Background

My name is Modupeoreoluwa, which means I thank God for God's gift. The short form and what everyone calls me is "Ore", meaning gift. I was born in Nigeria, West Africa and lived there all my life until I moved to the United States three years ago. I'm the eldest with a younger sister and brother. I graduated high school here in Chicago in 2015 and started college at Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago. I'm in my second year; currently majoring in Information Technology and Management. I do not have a specialization yet, but I'm thinking of going into cyber security. I'm also thinking of pursuing Masters coupled with my Bachelors in my current major. I see myself as a goal-oriented, curious, and easy-going person.

Interesting facts about me

I enjoy cooking, travelling, watching TV shows, exploring new things/places, and working with computers. I have visited 7 states in the U.S (Virginia, Colorado, Georgia, Maryland, Ohio, Michigan, and Purdue) and wish to visit more places! I have also visited few countries (Germany, France, Ghana, and South Africa).

Aspirations

I hope to manage my own company and use my degrees (Bachelors and Masters) to positively impact the community by providing a platform for young and curious youths to learn more about technology.
