Background About the Dish

Jollof rice is a very popular dish in many West African countries. It is known to be very delicious because of the ingredients used to prepare the dish. It is usually served at special occassions such as weddings, birthday parties, and so on. It can be eaten with chicken, beef, fish, alone, or anything desired. There are several methods to make the dish and still taste delectable. If you are curious on how to make this dish...look no further, relax, and learn from this step-by-step instructions!

Ingredients

  • The ingredients needed depends on the size of food to be cooked:

    • 3/4 cups of rice (118.5g)
    • 2 pieces of ripe tomatoes
    • 1 tin of tomato paste (optional)
    • 4 pieces of red ball pepper
    • 2 pieces of habanero pepper
    • 1 big onion
    • Ginger/garlic (optional)
    • Shrimps
    • Ground chicken or turkey
    • Mixed vegetables (optional)
    • Salt
    • Thyme
    • Curry powder
    • Knorr Chicken Flavor Bouillon Cubes (4)
    • Fried/grilled chicken
    • Chicken broth/stock
    • Vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Cut the peppers, tomatoes, onions, and garlic/ginger into small bits and pour into the blender
  2. Put the blender on low; don't fully blend, but make sure there are no big chunks of pepper
  3. Measure 3/4 cups of rice and rinse well to remove starch, then pour into a sieve to drain water
  4. Pour one cooking spoon of oil into the pot and place on gas stove with medium heat
  5. Cut onions into small bits and pour into the pot when the oil is a little hot, just to add flavor
  6. Carefully pour the blended pepper into the pot and spread evenly with spoon
  7. Add the necessary spices: chicken broth, curry powder, thyme, knorr chicken cubes
  8. Stir the mixture and cover the pot. Let it cook for about 10 mins
  9. Now pour the sieved rice into the pot; be careful so as not to splash oil on skin!!!
  10. Add enough water just a little bit above the rice level and mix together
  11. Cover the pot and let the rice cook
  12. Check the rice every 5-10 mins to make sure there's enough water to cook it
  13. Taste the rice and add more spices if not enough
  14. While the rice is cooking, fry the shrimps, ground turkey/chicken, and the mixed vegetables
  15. When the rice is getting soft with little/no water, add the shrimps and others
  16. In case the rice color isn't red enough, add some tomato paste and mix well
  17. Cover the pot and let the rice simmer for about 10 mins with very low heat
  18. Finally, after about 30-40 mins, the rice should be properly done with no water

Serving

The rice is finally done! Congratualtions on making jollof rice. It can be eaten with fried/grilled chicken, or beef or any choice of desired sides.
